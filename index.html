ABOUT ME

I've always been interested in optimization and research, as well as the human experience. Through working in market research I was able to watch customers give feedback on websites and then see for myself how companies would implement the feedback, and while I was a part of one side of the change, I wanted to be a part of the implementation side of the change. It was so gratifying to hear clients express how much happier people are using their website and how they're getting what they want out of it.
I wanted to be on the side of change so much so that at that job where I was a Research Assistant, when asked where I saw myself in my future career, I said I wanted to do programming. Less than a week later I was programming on SurveyGizmo, learning scripting and working closely with our programming team. Just a month or so later programming became my main job at the company, and almost a year after starting programming there I entered a full-time coding bootcamp to make programming my career.
Throughout my life I've always loved problem solving. I love anything data-based and science-driven. Every day there are small challenges to be solved that give me that same feeling of being accomplished, and again, cool, and I'm very excited to embark on a career change that allows me to solve problems and make positive changes.
